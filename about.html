ABOUT AGENCY

We are licensed professionals who arrange real estate transactions, putting buyers and sellers together and acting as their representatives in negotiations. We usually are compensated completely by a commission—a percentage of the property's purchase price, so their income depends on their ability to get a deal closed.
We usually specialize in either commercial or residential real estate. Either way, they perform different duties, depending on whether they work for the buyer or the seller.We also advise clients on how to price the property and prepare it for a sale, including providing tips on last-minute improvements that can boost the price or encourage speedy offers. we also market the property through listing services, networking, and advertisements.
It’s important for consumers to understand whether a we represents the buyer, the seller, or both parties; obviously,Our loyalty can greatly affect several details of the transaction, including the final price.We also disclose their representation so that buyers and sellers are aware of any conflicts of interest.
